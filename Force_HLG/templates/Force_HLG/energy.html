Review

\(\mathbf{Kinetic \text{ } energy}\) is the energy of an object due to its \(\color{red}\mathbf{MOTION}\)

\(\mathbf{Potential \text{ } energy}\) is the energy stored in an object due to its \(\color{green}\mathbf{POSITION}\)

\(\mathbf{Work}\) done by friction is the \(\color{Tan}\mathbf{ENERGY}\) \(\color{Tan}\mathbf{LOSS}\) due to \(\color{Tan}\mathbf{FRICTION}\) between the surface of the moving object and the floor.
